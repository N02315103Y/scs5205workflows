Scrum Methodology
Scrum is an Agile methodology used for managing and organizing complex software development projects. It is an iterative and incremental framework that focuses on delivering value to customers in short iterations called sprints.
DevOps (CI/CD)
DevOps promotes shared responsibilities, a culture of learning from failures, and the use of infrastructure as code. Continuous delivery enables the rapid and frequent release of software to production environments.
Quality Assurance & Testing
Test-driven development (TDD) and behavior-driven development (BDD) are methodologies that promote early and continuous testing. Automated testing helps improve testing efficiency and repeatability.

This course gives the knowledge and skills to choose the most appropriate methodology for various software development scenarios, adapt to changing requirements, collaborate effectively with team members, and deliver software solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and customer satisfaction.
